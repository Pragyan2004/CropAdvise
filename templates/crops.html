Crop Information

Learn about the different crops our system can recommend

Rice

Staple food for a large part of the world's population, especially in Asia.

Maize

Also known as corn, used for human consumption, animal feed, and biofuel.

Cotton

Grown for its fiber, used in textile industry.

Coconut

Tropical tree crop valued for its fruit and other products.

Apple

Popular temperate fruit tree grown for its sweet, pomaceous fruit.

Banana

Herbaceous plant grown for its fruit, important food crop worldwide.

Grapes

Grown for fruit, wine production, and raisins.

Mango

Tropical stone fruit known as the "king of fruits" in some regions.

Kidneybeans

Legume crop grown for its edible beans, rich in protein.

Chickpea

Legume valued for its nutrient-rich seeds.

Coffee

Tropical shrub grown for its beans, used to make coffee beverage.

Jute

Fiber crop used for making burlap, hessian, or gunny cloth.
